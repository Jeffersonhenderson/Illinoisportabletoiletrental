Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I know what kind of porta potty my event needs?

Think about your guests. For a rugged, informal event, our Neighborhood Essential is perfect. For an event where you want to show more care (like for families or older guests), the Courteous Plus is a great choice. For a luxury experience, the Ambassador Suite is the only answer.

2. Are your units actually clean? What’s your process?

Yes. This is our obsession. Every unit is power-washed, sanitized, and deodorized between rentals. We use a multi-point checklist to ensure it’s delivered in pristine condition, fully stocked and ready to go.

3. What is the real porta potty rental cost per day?

The porta potty price depends on the model you choose, how long you need it, and your location. A basic one day portable toilet rental is our most affordable option. We provide clear, all-inclusive quotes with no hidden fees.

4. I’m planning a wedding. Is a porta potty at a wedding tacky?

Not when it's done right. A "tacky" porta potty is one that's dirty and an afterthought. Our Courteous Plus units or our luxury portable bathroom rental trailers are designed to be so clean and comfortable that they become a positive, seamless part of the guest experience.

5. How far in advance should I reserve my rental?

For standard units, a week or two is often fine. For specialty units like our mobile restroom trailer or for large events during peak season, we recommend booking 4-6 weeks out to guarantee availability.

6. What's the difference between you and a big national chain like United Rentals porta potty?

Service. We are a local business that lives in this community. Our reputation is everything. You get personalized attention, direct communication, and a team that is deeply invested in the success of your event.
